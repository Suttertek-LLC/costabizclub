About Costa Biz Club

This group is all about CDS entrepreneurs and anyone offering services, coming together to support one another.

We share tips, resources, referrals, and encouragement. Whether you’re just starting out or have been in business for years, this is a space to connect, collaborate, and grow together.

We meet monthly at The Bistro on the second Friday of every month at 8:30 AM.

Let’s keep the vibe positive, respectful, and helpful!
